Which One Should You Use?

🎯 Use ChatGPT if you...

Need image generation, use voice mode, want access to plugins and GPTs, or need the most recognized AI brand for client work.

🎯 Use Claude if you...

Work with long documents, prioritize writing quality, need precise instruction-following, or want fewer AI hallucinations in professional settings.

💼 For Business

Claude wins for document analysis, legal review, and complex writing. ChatGPT wins for creative campaigns and customer-facing content.

👨‍💻 For Developers

Both are excellent. ChatGPT has more integrations. Claude writes cleaner, more maintainable code and gives better code explanations.

🏆 Our Verdict

Both are world-class AI assistants. ChatGPT is the better all-rounder with more features. Claude is better for writing, analysis, and working with long documents. Many professionals use both depending on the task.
