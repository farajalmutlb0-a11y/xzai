❓ Advertising FAQ

How quickly can my ad go live?
For display ads and newsletter placements, we can go live within 48–72 hours of payment. For sponsored articles, we typically need 5–7 business days to research, write, and optimize the content for SEO.
Do you write the sponsored article for me?
Yes! Our team writes all sponsored content. We'll ask you for product details, key messages, and your target keywords. You'll get a draft to review before publication. You can also submit your own article which we'll optimize and publish.
Are the articles labeled as sponsored?
Yes, all sponsored content is clearly labeled "Sponsored" or "Advertiser Content" in compliance with FTC guidelines. This maintains trust with our audience while clearly disclosing the commercial relationship.
What's the minimum ad spend?
Our minimum engagement is a single sponsored post at $350. For new advertisers, we recommend starting with the Newsletter + Site Bundle at $800/week to test results before committing to a monthly partnership.
Do you offer refunds?
We don't offer refunds once content is published or newsletter campaigns are sent. However, if we fail to deliver within the agreed timeline, we'll provide a full refund or credit. We stand behind our work 100%.
